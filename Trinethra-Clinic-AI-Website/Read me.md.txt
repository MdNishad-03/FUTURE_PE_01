# AI Website Copy Generator – Medical Clinic

## Business Chosen
Trinethra Skin and Ortho Care  
Location: Chikkamagaluru, Karnataka

## Problem
Local clinics often have unclear or generic website content, which reduces patient trust and appointment conversions.

## Solution
Used prompt engineering with AI to generate clear, professional, patient-friendly website copy for a real clinic.

## Tool Used
- Lovable (AI Website Builder)

## What AI Generated
- Homepage copy with strong value proposition
- Services content for dermatology and orthopaedics
- Trust-building sections
- Conversion-focused CTAs

## Outcome
- Real clinic website
- Publish-ready medical copy
- Reusable prompt system for other clinics

## Skills Demonstrated
- Prompt engineering
- AI website building
- UX writing for healthcare
- Conversion-focused copywriting
